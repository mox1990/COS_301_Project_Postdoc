\section{Getting Started}
\subsection{Welcome}
Welcome to the Post-Doctoral Application Management System, your first step to becoming a research fellow at the University of Pretoria. In a matter of minutes you will be able to submit your application for consideration. Once that is done you will be able to track the progress of your application, as well as receive updates. 

\subsection{Registering A New User}

\subsection{Logging On}
After registering with the system, you will be sent an email to verify that the email used to register is valid and yours. This email will contain a link to the Web Site.

To Log in:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Start your web browser and type the url in the address box
\item On the login page enter your user name or email address
\item Enter your password
\item Click Login
\item Depending on your security level you will see different information on this page.
\end{enumerate}}
